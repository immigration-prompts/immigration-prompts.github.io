# Instructions (prompt) for AI – Biographical Section for Recommendation Letters
1. Upload public information about the recommender (LinkedIn profile, resume)  
2. Copy/paste the following into your AI input. Word count will depend on the recommender and desired letter length. 100 words might be short. 300 words might be long. Be mindful that you'll have to edit the AI's output (often about 20%).


## The AI prompt

Please summarize this person's experience in 150-200 words, in the first person. Emphasize successes and achievements, establishing them as a credible authority in their field.
